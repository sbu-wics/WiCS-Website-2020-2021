Women in Computer Science

Stony Brook University

We are an organization whose mission is to provide encouragement and support to women, both graduate and undergraduate, in the field of computing. We aim to foster understanding between men and women in Computer Science and to improve women's involvement in the field. We hold events, both academic and social in nature, and work with other groups and the Computer Science department.

We are an official ACM-W chapter.

WiCSter of the Month

Jamie Kunzmann

Jamie is a senior graduating with a double major in Computer Science and Applied Math & Statistics. She has developed an interest in teaching while at Stony Brook, having been a TA for four courses. One of her favorite events with WiCS has been Hour of Code, during which they taught coding to local 5th graders. A highlight of her undergraduate experience was attending the Grace Hopper Celebration, the largest conference for women technologists. In her free time, she enjoys playing various sports (soccer, skiing, pickleball, volleyball, etc.), hiking, and watching Game of Thrones. After graduation she will begin working as a Software Engineer at Bloomberg.attended events and general body meetings throughout the semester.
